3. ADDED 7 NEW RATIO METHODS to app/calculators/ratios.py

   Profitability:
   - gross_profit_margin(gross_profit, total_revenue)  → Gross Profit / Total Revenue
   - ebit_margin(ebit, total_revenue)                  → EBIT / Total Revenue
   - roce(ebit, total_equity, total_debt)              → EBIT / (Equity + All Liabilities)
     Note: total_debt must include current liabilities so denominator = Total Assets.

   Efficiency:
   - fixed_asset_turnover(total_revenue, fixed_assets) → Total Revenue / Fixed Assets
   - days_sales_outstanding(accounts_receivable, total_revenue) → (AR / Revenue) * 365

   Valuation (new section):
   - eps(net_income, shares_outstanding)               → Net Income / Shares Outstanding
   - pe_ratio(market_price, eps)                       → Market Price / EPS

   Each method has: docstring, ValueError guard on zero denominator, round(result, 2).
